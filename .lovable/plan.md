

# Liora Restaurant Website Plan
*"Where Every Meal Shines"*

---

## Overview
A warm, cozy, and inviting multi-page website for Liora restaurant featuring a beautiful homepage and a comprehensive menu page with search functionality. The design will use warm colors (reds, oranges, cream, and earthy tones) to create a homey atmosphere.

---

## Page Structure

### 1. Homepage
- **Header Navigation** - Logo placeholder, navigation links (Home, Menu, About, Contact), and WhatsApp order button
- **Hero Section** - Large welcoming banner with the Liora logo, tagline "Where Every Meal Shines", and prominent "Order on WhatsApp" button
- **Menu Preview** - Visual cards showcasing the 5 categories (Cool Drinks, Desserts, Veg, Non-Veg, Tea & Coffee) with "View Full Menu" links
- **About Us Section** - Story of Liora, explaining the meaning of the name (Light) and the restaurant's warmth-focused philosophy
- **Contact Section** - Visual contact form (Name, Email, Message, Location dropdown) + embedded Google Map + contact details with phone: 91+xxxxxxxxxx
- **Footer** - Copyright info, phone number (91+xxxxxxxxxx), quick links, and social icons

### 2. Menu Page
- **Sticky Category Navigation** - Clickable category tabs that scroll to each section
- **Search Bar** - Real-time filtering of menu items by name
- **Category Sections** with all your provided items:
  - **Cool Drinks** (5 items): Coke, Pepsi, Lemonade, Mojito Mocktail, Iced Tea
  - **Desserts** (5 items): Chocolate Cake, Vanilla Ice Cream, Brownie, Gulab Jamun, Fruit Custard
  - **Veg Dishes** (5 items): Paneer Butter Masala, Veg Fried Rice, Dal Tadka, Mixed Veg Curry, Veg Noodles
  - **Non-Veg Dishes** (5 items): Chicken Biryani, Tandoori Chicken, Butter Chicken, Fish Fry, Mutton Curry
  - **Tea & Coffee** (5 items): Masala Tea, Ginger Tea, Cappuccino, Latte, Espresso
- Each item displays: Name, description, price, and placeholder food image
- Hover effects on menu cards for interactivity

## Design Elements

### Color Palette
- **Primary**: Warm terracotta/burnt orange (#D35400)
- **Secondary**: Cream/warm white (#FDF5E6)
- **Accent**: Deep burgundy (#8B0000)
- **Neutral**: Warm brown tones

### Visual Features
- Cozy, homey typography (rounded, friendly fonts)
- Soft shadows and warm gradients
- Food photography placeholders for each menu item
- Decorative elements like subtle patterns or food illustrations
- Smooth scroll animations

---

## Interactive Features
- **WhatsApp Integration** - All "Order on WhatsApp" buttons link to `https://wa.me/91xxxxxxxxxx`

- **Category Navigation** - Click to smoothly scroll to sections
- **Hover Effects** - Menu cards lift and highlight on hover
